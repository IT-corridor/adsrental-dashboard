Facebook Ads Account Manager

Looking for someone to help recruit new Facebook users for our ad campaigns.

This is an exciting opportunity for someone who is organized, ambitious and has experience with network marketing or recruiting. We offer a flexible schedule with the opportunity to earn 6 figures. We will provide training, guidance and support, as well as the opportunity to gain valuable work experience.

Who we are:

We are an affiliate marketing advertising company specializing in paid Facebook Ad Campaigns. We advertise products for many different companies primarily health, fitness and beauty industry.

Responsibilities:

  • Recruit quality Facebook users to participate in our program.
  • Manage your accounts to make sure they remain online and have the correct password.
  • Other responsibilities as assigned and/or as needed.

Our Process:

  1. Find a qualified user who is interested in participating in our program to rent us the ads portion of their FB account (separate than their personal FB) business.facebook.com - every FB account has this.
  2. Have them fill out our application with their basic information, login credentials, and where to send the check.
  3. Have the user download Splashtop and send them to our techs to connect with them remotely through Splashtop to verify the user qualifies.
  4. Once the user is qualified, we will send them our Adbox for the ads via USPS. This connects plugs in directly to the users modem/router. The user will need to leave this plugged in, and not change their PW for the duration of the ads.
  5. You will be paid for each account that connects their box. Many account managers build a team under them of recruiters who bring accounts in to them to recruit accounts.

Additional Information:

  • Position is 1099 independent contractor position
  • You do not have a set schedule but we do expect updates to account issues within 1 hour during normal business hours.

Qualifications:

Must be highly motivated and self-driven. You do not need experience in the industry but need experience with FB groups, recruiting, sales, or MLM are a major plus

Job Types: 1099 Independent Contractor

Previous experience is a major plus
Please share any experience in the industry and any relevant work experience.
