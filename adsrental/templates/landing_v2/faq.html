FAQ’s

You might wish to know

When am I paid?

Checks are processed the first Monday of each month for the days you were online with the correct password for the previous month and shipped within two business days.

How much am I paid?

You are paid $15 per month prorated for your number of days online with the correct password. If you are offline or have a wrong password, you will not be paid for that day.

What are the risks involved?

There are no risks to you. You never have to pay anything and we will never ask you for your credit card information. We run paid ads in your business Facebook separate than your personal Facebook account.

What kind of ads do you run?

Most of the ads that we run our for health or fitness industries. Depending on the companies we are currently working with, each specific ad may vary.

Will Ads show up on anyone’s feed?

The ads we create are the same sponsored ads you may have noticed on the right hand side of your news feed. Facebook chooses who sees these ads with their advertisement algorithm based on people’s interest. Our ads will NOT directly target your friends.

How long does it take to receive my box?

Once you are qualified, on average, you can expect to receive your box within about a week. Boxes are shipped processed and shipped by First Class Mail by USPS within 1-4 business days on average. First class mail takes on average 1-3 days to be delivered.

What do I do with my box when I am finished with the ads?

When you finish your ads, you can simply discard of your Adbox. The adbox can only be used for our ads, so it will be of no use to you.

How long do the ads run?

Most ads run between 4-6 months but some ads can last much longer or be much shorter
