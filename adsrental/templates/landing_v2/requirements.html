Must have stable and reliable private high speed internet throughout the duration of the ads

1 Year Old Account

Your Facebook account must be at least 1 year old

Must live in the US

Must not be planning on moving to a new address during the 4-5 months while the study is active (doing so, will stop monthly payments).

Untouched Ad Account

A computer to use.

Must use a computer for us to check if you qualify(only about 10-15 minutes).
