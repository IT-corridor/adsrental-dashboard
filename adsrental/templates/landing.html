FAQs

All further questions can be answered by your Account Manager!

Will the ads show up on anyone's feed?

Absolutely not. The ads we create are the same sponsored ads you may or may not notice on the right hand side of your news feed. Facebook chooses who sees these ads with their advertisement algorithm based on people’s interests.

What are the risks involved?

There are no risks to the personal side of your account. We create a backend in order to separate your personal account. We create and use what is called an Ad Manager. To control their ad content, Facebook occasionally flag Ad Managers but this affects our ads only. If you want to run a promotion in the future, you can simply create a new one.

What kind of ads do you run?

The majority of our clients are small businesses in the heath, fitness, and lifestyle industries, but we also have clients that you may have heard of (this can be where the icons are) Our ad campaigns represent their products or services.
